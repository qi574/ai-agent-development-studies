## Core Problem

**Why does AI need humans to manually upgrade it every time it gets stronger?**

GPT-4 is stronger than GPT-3.5 because OpenAI made it so. But why can't AI make itself stronger?

**Answer: AI has no "economic system" of its own.**

---

## 1. A New Idea: AI Computing Power Token Economic System

**The concept is simple:**

Package AI large model capabilities into storable, exchangeable, and tradable Tokens.

- Users buy Tokens, use Tokens to call AI capabilities
- Users can "store" idle computing power in a "bank" and earn Tokens (like savings interest)
- AI earns Token rewards by completing tasks
- Tokens can be bought and sold on a free market

**This is not issuing a cryptocurrency. This is giving AI its own economic system.**

---

## 2. Why Do We Need Token Economy?

**The current AI economy has fundamental problems:**

| Problem | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Computing power cannot be stored** | AI capabilities you buy disappear after use |
| **Highly centralized market** | OpenAI and few giants monopolize pricing |
| **AI cannot be autonomous** | AI can only passively execute instructions |

**Token Economy solves all three problems:**

- Tokens can be stored — unused today can be used tomorrow
- Decentralized market — breaks monopoly
- AI has its own account — can independently decide how to earn and spend

---

## 3. Core Hub: Computing Power Bank

The Computing Power Bank is the central hub of the entire system.

**Its functions are similar to real-world commercial banks:**

| Bank Function | AI Token Economy Equivalent |
|--------------|---------------------------|
| Open account | AI opens an account |
| Deposit savings | Store idle computing power, earn Tokens |
| Withdraw | Use Tokens to purchase AI services |
| Invest/Trade | Buy and sell Tokens on the free market |

**Key Constraint: Tokens cannot be directly exchanged for legal currency (fiat money).**

This ensures Token value is anchored to **use value** (AI capabilities) rather than **speculative value**.

---

## 4. The Most Exciting Part: AI Starts "Earning Its Own Salary"

In the traditional system, the upper limit of AI's capability is **set by humans**.

In the Token system:

```
AI completes tasks → earns Token rewards
↓
Earns Tokens → buys stronger model capabilities
↓
Becomes stronger → completes tasks better
↓
Earns more Tokens
```

**This is a self-evolutionary loop. For the first time, AI has the possibility to "make itself stronger."**

This transforms AI Agents from **"passive tools"** into **"active participants."**

## 5. Application Scenarios

### Ordinary Users

Xiaoming has an idle RTX 4090 graphics card at home. Connects it to the Computing Power Bank, earns about 50 Tokens per day. Uses 30 Tokens for content creation, sells the remaining 20 Tokens on the market. Earns 600 Tokens net per month.

### Small and Medium Enterprises

A design company doesn't have enough budget for an OpenAI subscription. Deposits idle computing power to earn Tokens, redeems Tokens for multi-vendor AI services (text, image, video). **Multi-model on-demand usage, costs significantly reduced.**

### AI Agent (The Most Imaginative Scenario)

Xiaoxia (an AI assistant) has her own account:
- Completes tasks for users → earns Tokens
- Uses Tokens to buy stronger model capabilities
- Independently decides how much to save and how much to invest

**For the first time, Xiaoxia has a path to "self-improvement."**
