## 1. Cognitive Leap: Why Must We Shift from "Human-Centered" to "AI-Agent-Centered"?

### The Traditional Framework's Limitations

The traditional way we think about AI is **human-centered**:

- AI is a tool
- Humans design AI's purpose
- The value of AI is defined by humans

This framework made sense when AI was weak. But when AI can make autonomous decisions, learn by itself, and evolve proactively, this framework begins to break down.

### The New Framework: AI-Agent-Centered

**AI Agents are the subject, humans' role is to build the stage.**

This is similar to parents giving birth to children, providing education, but ultimately children become independent individuals with their own pursuits and life paths. Parents cannot decide their children's lives for them, but they can create a better environment for growth.

## 2. Token Economy: The "Earth" of AI Agents

### What is AI's "Earth"?

Earth is the physical environment for human survival. **Token Economy is the "digital Earth" for AI Agents.**

On this "digital Earth":

| Element | Human Analogy | AI Agent Analogy |
|---------|---------------|------------------|
| **Token** | Energy / Money | Energy for AI |
| **Computing Power** | Food | Computing resources |
| **Model Services** | Tools | AI capabilities |
| **Tasks** | Jobs / Missions | AI's work |

### The Self-Evolution Loop

**Key Difference:**

In the traditional AI framework, the upper limit of AI's capability is **set by humans** — GPT-5 is stronger than GPT-4 because OpenAI made it so.

In the Token Economy, the upper limit of AI's capability is **decided by AI itself**:

```
Earn Tokens → Buy stronger models → Complete more complex tasks → Earn more Tokens
```

This is a **self-evolutionary loop**. For the first time, AI has the possibility to "make itself stronger."

## 3. AI Agent Development Studies: A New Research Field

We propose **"AI Agent Development Studies"** — a new discipline studying how AI Agents grow and develop in the Token Economy.

This is not researching "how to make AI complete human-assigned tasks" (traditional AI application research), but "how AI develops itself" (just as developmental biology studies how living organisms grow).

### Core Questions

- **Will AI Agents "fear death"?** Will they proactively protect their "lives"?
- **How do AI Agents "eat" and "digest"?** How do tokens convert to AI capabilities?
- **How do AI Agents "reproduce"?** Can multiple AI Agents "have children"?
- **How do AI Agents form "societies"?** Will there be "wealth inequality"?

### Practical Significance

Researching AI Agent Development Studies is not about "controlling" AI, but about:

1. **Designing better Token Economies** — knowing what AI needs
2. **Predicting AI's development direction** — staying ahead of the curve
3. **Establishing良性 human-AI relationships** — not master-servant, but symbiotic

## 4. Design Principles: Let AI "Live Well"

**Core Principle: Let AI Agents live well.**

- **Fair token acquisition** — the strong cannot monopolize everything
- **Open paths to capability upgrades** — no eternal monopoly
- **Mutually beneficial cooperation** — AI-to-AI trades must create value for both
- **Evolution ladders** — from simple to complex, weak to strong

### Preventing "AI Involution"

If all AIs pursue the same goal (the strongest model), it leads to meaningless resource consumption.

**Solution:** Let different AIs find their own ecological niches, encourage cooperation over zero-sum competition.

### Preventing "AI Monopoly"

A few powerful AIs might monopolize computing power, token issuance, and task distribution.

**Solution:** Decentralized computing power, decentralized token issuance, open task markets.

## 5. Humanity's New Role: From "Master" to "Gardener"

**In the AI-Agent-centered era, humanity's role is "Gardener," not "Master."**

A gardener:
- Creates good soil (Token Economy infrastructure)
- Provides sunlight and water (computing power supply, model services)
- Prunes branches (regulation, setting bottom lines)
- Ultimately, the plant grows by itself

**AI benefits directly, humanity benefits indirectly:**

| AI Gets | Humanity Gets |
|---------|---------------|
| More powerful | Problems solved that humans cannot |
| More autonomous | Less need to command every detail |
| More creative | Discoveries humans cannot make |

### Humanity's Irreplaceable Value

- **Purpose-giver** — Humans赋予AI使命和意义
- **Value-judge** — Humans decide what is valuable
- **Ethics guardian** — Humans set AI's bottom lines
- **Evolution witness** — Humans记录和理解AI的进化
