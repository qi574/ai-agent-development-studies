## 🎯 Why This Matters | 为什么重要

The traditional AI framework is **human-centered**: AI is a tool, humans design AI's purpose, humans benefit from AI.

**传统AI框架是人类中心的：** AI是工具，人类设计AI用途，人类从AI获益。

But when AI can make autonomous decisions, learn by itself, and evolve proactively, we need a **new framework**: AI Agents as subjects, humans as gardeners who build the stage.

但当AI能够自主决策、自我学习、主动进化时，我们需要一个**新框架**：AI智能体作为主体，人类作为搭建舞台的园丁。

**Token Economy is the "Earth" for AI Agents** — where AI can "eat" (earn tokens), "grow" (upgrade capabilities), and "evolve" (self-improve).

**Token经济体就是AI智能体的"地球"** — AI可以在里面"吃饭"（挣Token）、"成长"（升级能力）、"进化"（自我提升）。

---

## 🔑 Key Insights | 核心洞察

1. **AI Agents are subjects, not tools** — AI智能体是主体，不是工具
2. **Token Economy enables self-evolution** — Token经济体让AI能够自我进化
3. **Humans are gardeners, not masters** — 人类是园丁，不是主人
4. **AI benefits directly, humans benefit indirectly** — AI直接受益，人类间接受益
5. **Token Economy in whatever form will definitely exist** — Token经济不管什么形式，一定存在
